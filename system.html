In this system, the state variables are: \(\omega_{pole}\), \(\alpha_{pole}\), \(\omega_{cart}\), and \(\alpha_{cart}\). Here, \(\omega\) represents angular velocity and \(\alpha\) represents angular accelleration. The carts accelleration and velocity are relative to the axis centered at the center of the circular ramp.


Equations for states of system:

  • \(\frac{d \omega_{pole}}{dt} = \alpha_{pole} \)
