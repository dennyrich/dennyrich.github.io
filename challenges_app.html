Challenges Game

The app where you can join groups and together, complete challenges. See who can complete the game first by reaching the end of the grid, where each square has a small subset of the group's challenges. To reach the end, you can move left, right, or up each day to a square by completing any challenge that is on that square. To see your own progress, the app will have fun and informative visualations.
Resources: React-Native, Unsplash, Redux, Expo, Stack-Overflow
